Teleportals - Final project by Daniel Cossu

**CONTROLS**

Forward      - W
Backward     - s
Strafe Left  - A
Strafe Right - D

Pick up objects - E

Look around with the mouse

Left Click shoots one portal
Right click shoots the other

F12 will take a screenshot

The models were created by Tobias @ BlendSwap. Check out his awesome models at (http://www.blendswap.com/blends/author/gamerfreaq/)
